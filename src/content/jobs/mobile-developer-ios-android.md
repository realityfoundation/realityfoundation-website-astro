## Must-have technical skills

- Native iOS (Swift) and/or Android (Kotlin) development experience; cross-platform (Flutter or React Native) is acceptable with demonstrated native depth
- Hands-on experience with device integrity mechanisms, specifically Apple DeviceCheck/App Attest and Google Play Integrity API
- Experience integrating camera hardware APIs and handling raw media at capture time
- Familiarity with mobile cryptographic operations (hashing, signing, secure enclave usage)
- Understanding of secure data transfer between device and backend
- Experience shipping production apps, ideally in regulated or security-sensitive contexts

## What you bring

- You take ownership of the user journey end to end, from capture interaction to certified record
- You are rigorous about security edge cases and treat them as product-critical
- You can operate independently and make sound architectural decisions under ambiguity
- You care about building something societally useful, not only shipping features quickly

## What you'll do

- Build and maintain the RealityCheck capture app for iOS and Android
- Implement and maintain device integrity verification flows (DeviceCheck/App Attest, Play Integrity)
- Own the pipeline from camera capture to cryptographic hash generation and backend submission
- Collaborate with backend and security engineers on the media-to-certificate flow
- Ensure the app handles user data responsibly and aligns with GDPR-by-design principles
