## What you bring — technical and domain skills

- Experience in B2B strategy, business development, or institutional partnerships, ideally in insurtech, govtech, media, or adjacent sectors
- Ability to build and manage a commercial pipeline from cold outreach to signed agreement
- Familiarity with the European regulatory landscape, particularly the EU AI Act and data sovereignty frameworks
- Comfort with structured data and market sizing; you can build an argument from evidence, not only intuition
- Experience presenting to institutional or enterprise buyers is strongly preferred

## What you bring — as a person

- You can adapt your communication style across insurers, public agencies, media organizations, and civil society partners
- You are comfortable with ambiguity and can structure a strategy where no playbook exists
- You have a clear position on why media authenticity matters and can advocate it credibly
- You are rigorous: you flag uncertainty, verify assumptions, and challenge narratives that are not data-backed

## What you'll do

- Own the expansion pipeline across our six target segments: insurers, government agencies, news organizations, marketing agencies, NGOs, and consumers
- Identify and prioritize partnership and pilot opportunities, starting with insurance
- Lead outreach and relationship management with institutional partners and prospective clients
- Contribute to grant and funding applications aligned with our non-profit structure
- Work directly with the founding team to refine go-to-market positioning and segment priorities
