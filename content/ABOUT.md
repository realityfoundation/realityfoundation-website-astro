## Our mission

RealityFoundation is an independent nonprofit research foundation established in Estonia and operating across Europe. Our mission is to advance the scientific understanding of AI-generated counterfeits — synthetic images, video, and audio produced with the intent to deceive — and to develop the knowledge base needed to detect, prevent, and mitigate their impact on society.

We believe that the integrity of visual reality is a public good. As generative AI capabilities expand rapidly, the gap between what is real and what is fabricated is becoming impossible to close without dedicated, rigorous, independent research. RealityFoundation exists to close that gap.

---

## What we do

We operate as a platform for academic research, policy analysis, and public engagement. Our work spans three areas:

- **Research:** We commission, support, and publish original research on fake detection algorithms, synthetic media forensics, and the real-world consequences of AI counterfeiting.
- **Outreach:** We communicate our findings to policymakers, journalists, insurers, and the broader public through accessible publications, briefings, and events.
- **Community:** We bring together researchers, students, and practitioners working on the frontiers of media authenticity and AI governance.
